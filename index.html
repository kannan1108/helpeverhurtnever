The Teaching

Somewhere along the way, we confused busyness with purpose. We built systems that reward cruelty and call it efficiency. We built diets that poison us and call them normal. We built wars and called them necessary.

Help Ever. Hurt Never. is not a correction.
It is a remembering.

You already know this. You have always known this. The question is whether you are willing to live it — in what you eat, what you wear, what you build, what you say when no one is watching.

This is the work. This is the only work.
